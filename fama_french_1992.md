# FAMA FRENCH 1992



## Key Result 

Question: Why do smaller, underpriced companies consistently outperform the market when traditional finance theory says risk is determined by beta alone?
Method: TBD
Key Result: Beta alone doesn't explain returns; size and book-to-market do
Why It Mattered: It permanently altered investors' evaluation metrics. Fama and French showed CAPM was incomplete and extended it with size and value
One Critique: Despite its brilliance, the wonderous fact is that this model fails to explain the massive outperformance of unprofitable, high-growth tech giants over the last decade.
